FP3: Norris leads Hamilton and Russell in final practice ahead of Mexico City Grand Prix

Lando Norris was fastest in Free Practice 3 as his title rivals struggled to match his pace in Mexico.

13 hours ago
IMAGE: F1 CAR — MEXICO CITY GP 2025

Norris posted a time of 1:16.505 to top the final practice session, finishing 0.226s clear of Lewis Hamilton, while George Russell was third fastest for Mercedes.

Championship leader Max Verstappen could only manage fourth, 0.359s off the pace, raising questions about Red Bull's competitiveness on a track that has traditionally been one of their strongest.

Carlos Sainz rounded out the top five for Ferrari, with team-mate Charles Leclerc back in seventh behind Alpine's Pierre Gasly, who impressed once again with his pace.
